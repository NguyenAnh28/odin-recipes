Recipe for Beef Mince Stirfry

A very simple yet delicious and nutritious recipe that will put a smile on anyone's face when eaten with a bowl of hot rice. Very easy and quick to make, a go-to for busy adults and broke students

Ingredients:

  • Mince Beef: 200g
  • Vegetables of choice: 200g
  • Vegetable / Canola oil: 3 tablespoons
  • Spices of choice

Steps:

  1. Set a pan on medium high heat
  2. Prepare the ingredients: Vegetables washed and peeled, Beef at room temperature
  3. Pour in the oil and all of the protein, veggies
  4. Season with spices of choice
  5. Cook until most of the water are gone and veggies are cooked
  6. Serve with a hot bowl of rice
